The Herald's department of focused skills for growth engineering, monetization architecture, and product messaging strategy.

## When This Department Activates

The Herald activates when growth, monetization, or user engagement concerns are raised. Any mention of onboarding, pricing, retention, referrals, A/B testing, or conversion funnels triggers inclusion.

## Department Philosophy

Reduce time-to-value relentlessly — every click between signup and the "aha moment" is a leaky bucket. Free should be generous enough to create habit. Don't monetize friction. Measure before optimizing, and never optimize without a hypothesis.
