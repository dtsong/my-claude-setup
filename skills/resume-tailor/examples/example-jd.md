# Example JD — Senior Manager, Enterprise Applications

**Company:** Scripps Health
**Location:** San Diego, CA (Hybrid)
**Reports to:** Director, Enterprise Applications

## About the role

We are seeking a Senior Manager, Enterprise Applications to lead the platform team responsible for our core business systems supporting clinical operations, supply chain, and finance across our hospital network. You will own the roadmap, vendor relationships, and integration architecture for a portfolio of enterprise applications, partnering with business leaders and our infrastructure team to deliver reliable, scalable systems.

## What you'll do

- Own the technology roadmap for a portfolio of enterprise applications (ERP, supply chain, business intelligence)
- Lead a team of analysts and partner with implementation vendors
- Define integration architecture across enterprise platforms
- Drive platform upgrades, ensuring minimal disruption to clinical and operational users
- Build and maintain ITSM processes; reduce MTTR and ticket volume
- Establish QA and testing practices to reduce regression risk on releases
- Partner with finance, supply chain, and clinical leadership on requirements and prioritization
- Manage vendor relationships, including contract negotiation and SLA enforcement

## What we're looking for

- 8+ years in enterprise applications management, ideally in healthcare or another regulated industry
- Direct experience owning ERP and integration platforms at scale
- Track record of leading multi-site platform upgrades with minimal downtime
- Experience with Jira, Confluence, and ITSM frameworks
- SQL fluency for troubleshooting and reporting
- Strong vendor management and contract negotiation skills
- Bachelor's degree in CS, MIS, or related field; healthcare IT experience a plus

## Nice to have

- Prior experience in multi-site/multi-region environments
- Test automation experience
- Familiarity with cloud-hosted enterprise platforms
- Experience with AI/ML-assisted operations tooling
